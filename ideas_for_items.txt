- Generic Beer -
On attendance: applies one stack of Alcoholism to the course that you use it on, permanently halving the chance to understand it.

- Inverter -
On attendance: if your Chance of Understanding is below 50%, invert it.

- Epic Beer -
On attendance: gives you one Master Professor item. Has a 10% chance of applying Alcoholism to the course that you use it on, permanently halving the chance to understand it.

- Master Professor -
On attendance: Gives you 2^n Understandings where n is the amount of active Master Professors.

- A Window -
On use: Restarts your run with your current items.

- Grass -
On touch: adds 1 Procrastination.

- Cookie -
On use: gives you a cookie.

- Pot -
On attendance: gives you 1 item from a Mild Box.
