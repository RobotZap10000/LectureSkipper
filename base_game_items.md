
## COMMON

- [ ] AI Assistant - On Attend: Doubles understand chance but halves understandings gained.
- [x] Apple - On Attend: increase your max energy by x%.
- [x] Pizza - On Attend: lose x% less energy from that lecture.
- [x] Cookie - On Use: has a x% chance of giving you a cookie. Going over 100% can give you more cookies per use.
- [ ] Video Game - On Skip: gain +x% extra energy.

## RARE

- [ ] GPS - On Attend: You have a +x% chance of understanding this lecture.
- [ ] Hacking - On Attend: this lecture cannot appear the next x times. Can only be used once per block.
- [ ] Course Material - After Attend: permanently xx the amount of understandings that this lecture gives, but now it's xx less likely to appear. Can be used once per block.
- [ ] Brain - On Attend: if you understand this lecture, gain all the required Understandings. Otherwise, lose all Understandings. Cannot go past the goal understandings of the course. Can only be used once block.
- [x] Cart - After Attend: whenever this lecture appears, gain +x$. Can only be used once per block.

## LEGENDARY

- [x] Schedule Editor - On Attend: this lecture is now guaranteed to appear the next x times. Can only be used once per block.
- [ ] Time Machine - On Attend: Resets your understandings for this course, but adds +x lectures.
- [ ] Inverter - On Attend: if your Chance of Understanding is below x%, invert it.
